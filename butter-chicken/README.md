# Butter Chicken
A classic Indian curry, also known as Murgh Makhani

## Ingredients

### Marinade
- 1/2 cup plain yoghurt, full fat
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp tumeric powder
- 2 tsp garam masala (Note 1)
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper powder (Note 2)
- 1 tsp ground cumin / or hot chili 
- 1 tbsp ginger, freshly grated
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1.5 lb / `750g` chicken thigh fillets, cut into bite size pieces

### Curry
- 2 tbsp (30 g) ghee or butter, OR 1 tbsp vegetable oil (Note 3)
- hot chili 
- 1 cup tomato puree (Note 4)
- 1 cup heavy / thickened cream (Note 5)
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 1/4 tsp salt
