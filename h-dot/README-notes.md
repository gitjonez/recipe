For a 9 inch quiche I use the following pastry:

8 oz plain (all purpose) flour
5.5 oz cold butter in small cubes
1 large egg
Pinch of table (fine) salt
Water to mix

I pulse the flour, salt and cold cubed butter in the processor until it looks like breadcrumbs then tip into a bowl and slowly add the egg plus a small amount of water to make a dough that is pliable but not wet. This makes a very rich pastry which is good for savoury pies and quiche.
